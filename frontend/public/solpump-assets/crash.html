Game Guide: Crash (The Classic Crypto Crash Game)

Crash is the iconic, fast-paced experience of Web3 gaming. A multiplier rises from 1.00 upward until a "bust" moment instantly ends the round. Your goal is to cash out before it busts.

  3. You can cash out anytime-your payout equals your stake the multiplier at cashout.
  4. If the round busts before you cash out, your stake is lost for that round.

Crash outcomes are determined by logic anchored to on-chain inputs (and, where used, verifiable randomness). Each round produces a transparent result that can be matched to publicly visible data. Coinflip: Planning for Streaks You Will Actually See

A 50/50 flip in a blockchain casino still produces loss streaks; over time, four or five in a row will happen. Plan around that reality instead of reacting to it.
